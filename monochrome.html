Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary Palettes

Primary colors consist of red, yellow, and blue.

Secondary colors, created through a combination of primary colors, are orange, green, and purple.

Tertiary or intermediate colors are the mixtures of the other six colors, located in between the primary/secondary colors on the color wheel.
